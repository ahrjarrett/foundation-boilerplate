Dallas Medical LLC advocates on a global basis for the highest ethical standards, timely patient access to safe and effective products, and economic policies that reward value creation. Read more.

We’re stoked you want to try us!

Dallas Medical LLC is a privately held, Texas-based clinical service provider and medical device distributor. Since we got our start in 2011, we have been dedicated to bringing early-stage innovation from concept to implementation in clinical practice. We began our journey focusing on spine devices, but our reach has expanded to touch many different medical fields, and we have also developed a strong relationship with Boolean Laboratory, a leading facility in Texas for a wide variety of testing services.
